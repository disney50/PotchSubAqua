Welcome / Welkom...

Welcome to Potch Sub Aqua Underwater Hockey Club... The home of the North West Potchefstroom Provincial Underwater Hockey Teams... Where we play a breathtaking sport that leaves you breathless!

About us... / Oor ons...

The Potch Sub Aqua Underwater Hockey Club has been in existence since the 1970s and has contributed not only many Springbok players but also Springbok coaches from its ranks... Take a look at the Potch Sub Aqua Facebook Page for more about us...

Die Potch Sub Aqua Onderwater Hokkie Klub bestaan reeds sedert die 1970's en het nie net baie Springbok-spelers, maar ook Springbok-afrigters uit sy geledere bygedra... Gaan kyk na die Potch Sub Aqua Facebook Page vir meer oor ons...
